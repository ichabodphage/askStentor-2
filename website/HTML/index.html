what is askstentor?

askstentor is an original dictionary application providing words and terms commonly used within the plural community.

history

askstentor began as a small plural term discord bot, which quickly grew in size thanks to a demand for such an application.

since then the original bot has expanded onto the web and is now both a website and a discord application

rules regarding collection of terms

only staff of askStentor is allowed to directly add or remove terms within the bot. users of the application may request the addition of individual terms, provided they provide the name of the term, the deffiniton for said term, a short description of that term within a sentance, the category for said term, and have proof of either being the coiner or have concent from the coiner to add into the bot. staff may add terms without directly asking the individual coiners. however individual coiners may request the removal of the term from the application, provided they provide proof of coining said term.
